Telegram bot commands

Everything you can tell
the bot to do

Open the bot on Telegram and use these commands. That's all you need to know.

/startFirst thing to do

Introduces the bot and shows all available commands. Always start here.

What to type/start
/monitorMost important

Adds a competitor to watch. The bot will ask for the name, then optional extra keywords.

Step by step
1
Type /monitor and send
2
Bot asks: "Which competitor?" → type the name e.g. Notion
3
Bot asks for keywords → type /skip to use defaults or type custom ones
4
Done ✓ Bot confirms monitor is active
/testUse after setup

Runs a manual Reddit check right now — don't wait for the hourly cron. Use this to verify everything is working.

What to type/test
Takes ~10-15 seconds. Bot will reply with results or say "no new mentions".
/listCheck active

Shows all competitors currently being monitored along with their keywords.

What to type/list
Example reply: "1. Notion — tracking: notion, notion alternative, switched from notion"
/dashboardGet your ID

The bot replies with your unique Chat ID. You need this to log into the dashboard on this website.

Step by step
1
Type /dashboard and send
2
Bot replies with a number like 1234567890 — that's your Chat ID
3
Copy it → go to Dashboard → paste it in the input box

Compeddit is a Reddit competitor monitoring tool. You tell it which competitors to watch, and it scans Reddit every hour across posts, comments, and subreddit feeds. Every mention is classified by Groq AI as a buying signal, complaint, or comparison — then you get an instant Telegram alert so you can act before your window closes.

Compeddit runs an automated cron job every hour that hits 4 Reddit sources in parallel — new posts, comments, subreddit feeds, and keyword searches. Each result is passed through Groq AI which classifies intent (buying signal, complaint, comparison) and urgency before alerting you. High-urgency results are pinged to your Telegram immediately. You can also trigger a manual check anytime using the /test command.
