Research Overview

Innovation at a Glance

RentIt's model addresses one of India's most pressing digital inclusion challenges — formalised here as an IEEE innovation submission by Team RentIt.

Introduction

RentIt is a peer-to-peer computer and parts rental platform designed to democratise access to technology across India. The platform enables students, freelancers, designers, gamers, and small businesses to rent high-performance computers, laptops, and individual hardware components on a short-term basis — daily, weekly, or monthly — at prices starting at ₹99/day. Devices are delivered and picked up from the user's doorstep, fully tested, sanitized, and insured, removing every barrier between a user and the technology they need.

Problem Statement

Access to personal computing is not universal — it is a privilege. The key barriers are:

  • →High upfront cost. Average laptops cost ₹800+ and high-end workstations can run into tens of thousands — entirely out of reach for low-income users.
  • →Short-term need, long-term ownership. Students, freelancers, and project-based professionals need powerful hardware for weeks, not years — buying makes no financial sense.
  • →Unaffordable components. GPU or RAM upgrades required for creative or compute-intensive work remain inaccessible for most users.
  • →Growing digital divide. With 65% of India's 600M+ students unable to afford a laptop, this gap compounds educational and economic inequality.

Proposed Solution

RentIt — rent any computer or part you need, for as long as you need it, at a price you can afford.

  • ✓Full PC & parts rental — laptops, workstations, gaming rigs, and individual components like GPUs and RAM available on demand.
  • ✓Flexible duration — daily, weekly, or monthly plans starting at ₹99/day. No long-term contracts. Extend anytime.
  • ✓Doorstep delivery & pickup — zero travel required. Devices delivered within 48 hours, collected on schedule.
  • ✓Fully insured & sanitized — every device is tested, cleaned, and factory-reset on return. User data is always safe.

Advantages & Challenges

Advantages
  • +Eliminates high upfront costs
  • +Accessible to students & low-income users
  • +Flexible, pay-as-you-go model
  • +Reduces e-waste via device reuse
  • +₹12B+ addressable market in India
Challenges
  • −Logistics & last-mile delivery at scale
  • −Device damage & loss risk management
  • −Trust & data privacy perception
  • −Building initial device inventory
  • −Behaviour shift from buy to rent mindset

Conclusion

RentIt demonstrates that technology access need not require technology ownership. By combining a simple rental marketplace with doorstep logistics and flexible pricing, the platform creates a scalable, sustainable pathway to digital inclusion for India's 600M+ students and working professionals.

The vision is clear: technology should never be a privilege. RentIt is building the infrastructure to make that vision real — one rental at a time.
